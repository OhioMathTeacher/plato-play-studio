# Character Breakdown: Plato's Allegory of the Cave

## Recommended Casting: Ensemble Rotation (11+ Speaking Parts)

This approach gives each scene its own Socrates and Glaucon, creating 11+ distinct speaking roles while maintaining theatrical cohesion.

---

## Speaking Roles by Scene

### Scene 1: The Cave + Liberation (Triad A)
1. **SOCRATES I** - Narrator/philosopher, describes the cave allegory (major role)
2. **GLAUCON I** (GLAW-kon) - Dialogue partner, responds to Socrates (moderate role)
3. **FIGURES** - Shadow-makers who walk in front of the sheet (2-3 named individuals, minor speaking)

**Non-speaking roles:**
- PRISONERS (several, chained and watching shadows)
- SHADOW PUPPET OPERATORS (2-3 students behind the sheet with flashlights and cutouts)

---

### Scene 2: The Ascent (Triad B)
1. **SOCRATES II** - Narrator/philosopher, continues description (major role)
2. **GLAUCON II** - Dialogue partner in "Freedom, Stage Three: The Sun" section (moderate role)
3. **THEA** - The freed prisoner, reacts to light and freedom (major role)
4. **GUARD 1** - Releases Thea, speaks about reality vs. shadows (moderate role)
5. **GUARD 2** - Questions Thea about objects (minor role)

---

### Scene 3: The Return (Triad C)
1. **SOCRATES III** - Narrator/philosopher, describes the return (major role)
2. **GLAUCON III** - Dialogue partner, responds to Socrates (moderate role)
3. **THEA** - Returns to the cave (major role, carried over from Scene 2)

**Non-speaking roles:**
- PRISONERS (react silently, mock Thea)

---

## Complete Cast List (11-14 Speaking Parts)

### Major Speaking Roles (7)
1. SOCRATES I (Scene 1)
2. SOCRATES II (Scene 2)
3. SOCRATES III (Scene 3)
4. GLAUCON I (Scene 1)
5. GLAUCON II (Scene 2 - Stage Three only)
6. GLAUCON III (Scene 3)
7. THEA (Scenes 2 & 3)

### Supporting Speaking Roles (4-7)
8. GUARD 1 (Scene 2)
9. GUARD 2 (Scene 2)
10. FIGURE 1 - Shadow-maker (Scene 1, brief speaking)
11. FIGURE 2 - Shadow-maker (Scene 1, brief speaking)
12. FIGURE 3 - Shadow-maker (Scene 1, optional, brief speaking)

### Technical Roles (Non-speaking, but essential)
- SHADOW PUPPET OPERATORS (2-3 students behind sheet with flashlights/puppets)
- PRISONERS (ensemble, 3-5 students, physical acting only)

**Total: 12-15 speaking parts** ✓ Exceeds your goal!

---

## Notes for Casting

- **THEA appears in Scenes 2 & 3** - Same actor throughout
- **GLAUCON appears in ALL three scenes** - But different actors (GLAUCON I, II, III) for ensemble approach
- **Roman numerals match scene numbers:** SOCRATES I/II/III and GLAUCON I/II/III correspond to Scene 1/2/3
- **Dialogue tags in script:** Now show the scene number (SOCRATES I, GLAUCON II, etc.) for casting clarity
- **Stage directions specify:** Which numbered character is in which scene

This ensemble approach ensures:
- Every student has substantive dialogue
- No one is overwhelmed with too many lines
- Multiple students get to embody the philosophical voices
- Perfect for a class of 13 students!
